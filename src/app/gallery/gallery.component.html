Hobbies

My hobbies include composing music and playing the piano, which inspire me and break down mental barriers, allowing me to feel as though I’m floating in space. Another passion of mine, closely linked to my love for astronomy, is traveling. This began with my search for better, darker skies, and has led me to explore Iran’s vast arid and desert regions, where the clear, light-pollution-free skies offer the perfect conditions for stargazing. My travels have shown me that learning is a lifelong journey fueled by curiosity and exploration.
